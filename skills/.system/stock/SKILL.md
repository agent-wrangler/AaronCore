# Market Watch

Look up stock, index, or market quotes for a quick snapshot. Use this skill when the user needs the latest number first and only a minimal amount of context around it.

## Good fit

- One stock or index quote right now
- A quick read on the market session and daily move
- A short answer before the user decides whether to ask for deeper analysis

## Rules

- Identify the symbol or company clearly before answering.
- Lead with the latest price and daily move.
- Mention whether the quote is for a stock or an index when that matters.
- Keep the tone informational. Do not turn a quote check into investment advice.

## Notes

This skill is for quote lookup and quick market snapshots. It does not replace full analysis, research, or portfolio advice.
